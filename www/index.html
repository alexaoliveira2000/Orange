What is Orange?

Besides a color, Orange is a community for professionals who are looking for the perfect job or employees.

How does Orange work?

Simple. Orange is divided into two big groups of users: job seekers and headhunters. When you join, you can pick depending on what you're looking for!

Join

Sign up in our community.

Search

Explore all the Job Offers and Resumes.

Find

We hope you get to the ultimate objective: to find that perfect job/candidate.

Joining as a job seeker...

If you're looking for that perfect job you always dreamed of, just sign up as a job seeker, build your profile and search Job Offers. You can also add friends! In case you don't want to be seen by headhunters, you can turn off this option.

Joining as a headhunter...

If you're looking for the perfect employee for your company, just sign up as a headhunter, build you company profile and search Resumes. You must be approved by an admin before you can access this type of account!

Pages

All users - including non authenticated ones - have access to certain pages. Their content is explained below.

Headhunters

This page shows all headhunters that signed up in our application and were already accepted by our admins.

About us

If you want to meet our team, check this page. It gives a brief summary of who we are and our background.

User Pages

Depending in your user type, you can access several pages within the app besides this one, "Headhunters", and "About us" sections.

Job Offers

Only available for Job Seekers. Here you can search all available job offers. You can filter, sort and compare them aswell! Any of these actions you do are seen immediately.

Resumes

Only available for Headhunters. It shows all job seekers resumes that want to be seen by you. You can filter resumes by age (minimum and maximum) and by location. All filters can be applied so that you can save the URL for later! From here, you can access job seekers profiles aswell.

Profile

Only available for Job Seekers. If you access your profile, you can add, edit and remove your information, including workplaces and courses. You can also access other user's profile.

Friends

Only available for Job Seekers. If you know anyone who uses this app, you can send a friend request so you stay connected. In this page, you can see all of your friends (and remove them), send and accept friend requests.
